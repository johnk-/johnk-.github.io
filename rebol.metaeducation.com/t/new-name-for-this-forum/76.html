rgchris May 27, 2017, 6:44pm 19

You need a FreeDNS account.

  1. Create a FreeDNS account.

  2. Click 'Subdomains'

  3. Click 'Add'

  4. From the 'Domain' drop down, find the option to locate a new domain and search for 'rebol.info'

  5. Finish up the form

  6. Configure your web host to accept requests from rebol.info.

I think you get a quota of five subdomains without mandatory contribution subject to logging in every six months.
